About Us

Mozilla Club Zeal  |  Collaborate. Innovate. Share.

History

The story began in June 2015, when a bunch of enthusiastic students came together and formed  Zeal Firefox Club. The club aimed at following Mozilla's principles and developing an 'open' environment in the campus. The club conducted a host of awesome events including Firefox OS app development workshop and Privacy Awareness Workshop.

A year and a half later, Mozilla announced the end of Firefox Student Ambassadors program under which Zeal Firefox Club was established. This led to the inception of a new club with renewed energy and zeal -  Mozilla Club Zeal.

This day and age

Presently, Mozilla Club Zeal boasts of an active member group and zealous committee members. We help the students of Zeal College of Engineering and Research get started with Open Source contributions. We evangelise about the Open Web and help students acquire the 21st Century Skills so that they can succeed in todays's world.
